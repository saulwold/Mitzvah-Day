Mitzvah Day 2009

Sunday, April 26


Join the entire Jewish community to help others this spring! Volunteer just a little of your time and help support various projects around our community. This effort is a partnership between the Jewish Community Relations Council, TBI Sisterhood, Jewish Federation of Lane County, Temple Beth Israel, Or haGan, Ad Olam, Ahavas Torah, UO Hillel, AEPi Fraternity, and UO Jewish Student Union.

Mitzvah Day Kick-Off Breakfast

April 26th 8:45 a.m. at TBI

All participants are invited to the Kick-Off Breakfast and and will receive a Mitzvah Day T-Shirt.

 

Mitzvah Day Projects include (click project title for details):

Blankets for Needy Children

Beautification at Harris-Eastside Elementary

Gardening at Mt. Pisgah

Park Enhancement at Mt. Pisgah

Activities for Seniors at South Hill Rehab Center

Spring Cleaning at First Place Family Center

Toiletry Kits for Womenspace

Home Starter Kits for Homeless Families

Kards for Kids: Card and Book Making for Children at Neve Michael Children’s Village in Israel
